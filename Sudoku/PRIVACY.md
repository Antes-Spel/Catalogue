# Privacy Policy — Sudoku

**Last updated:** March 30, 2026

## Overview
Sudoku ("the App") is developed and published by **Andreas Olsson**. This privacy policy explains how we handle user data.

## Data Collection
**We do not collect any personal data.** The App:
- Does not require an account or login
- Does not track your location
- Does not use analytics or advertising SDKs
- Does not transmit any data to external servers
- Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

All game progress is stored **locally on your device** and is never transmitted.

## Third-Party Services
The App does not integrate any third-party services that collect data.

## Children's Privacy
The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. It is safe for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
