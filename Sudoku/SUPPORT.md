## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I change the difficulty?
Return to the main menu using the **←** button in the top-left corner, then select Easy, Medium, or Hard.

### What's the difference between difficulties?
- **Easy:** 25 cells removed, incorrect guesses are highlighted in red
- **Medium:** 40 cells removed, no error hints
- **Hard:** 55 cells removed, no error hints

### How do I start a new puzzle?
Tap and hold the **"✦ New Puzzle"** button below the board for 3 seconds. If you've already completed the current puzzle, a single tap is enough.

### How do I clear a cell?
Tap the cell to open the number wheel, then tap the center of the wheel to clear it.

### Is my data shared?
No. The app does not collect or share any data. See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md).
